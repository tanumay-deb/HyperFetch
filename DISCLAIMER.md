# Disclaimer & acceptable use

Smart Download Manager is a general-purpose download tool. Read this before
using or distributing it.

## Intended use
Download content that you have the right to download: your own files, files you
are licensed to access, public/openly-licensed media, and content whose terms
permit downloading.

## Your responsibilities
- **Respect copyright.** Do not use this software to copy or redistribute
  copyrighted material without permission.
- **Respect site terms of service.** Some sites prohibit downloading or
  automated access. Following those terms is your responsibility.
- **Do not circumvent DRM.** This tool does **not** break DRM (Widevine,
  FairPlay, PlayReady) and must not be used to attempt to. Encrypted streaming
  services (YouTube, Netflix, etc.) are out of scope and will not work.
- The HLS feature decrypts only standard **AES-128 HLS**, which is transport
  encryption that the playing client is already given the key for — it is not a
  DRM-circumvention tool.
